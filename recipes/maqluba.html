Description

Maqluba is a traditional Levantine dish, a variety of Pilaf[1] that is popular across Palestine, Jordan, Syria, Lebanon, and Iraq.

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Cauliflower
  • Potatoes
  • Fried Tomatoes
  • Eggplant

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Add spices
  3. Line a pot with vegetables
  4. Cover with rice and cook
